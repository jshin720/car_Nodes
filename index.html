About:

Car_node is a data visualization project, where the user can see the countries where car brands are from, cars from specific car brands, and information for a specific car.

Instructions:

Click on each node to get to the next level. Once you get to the car node level, click on the node to get more information on the specific car. You also zoom and drag within the graph window. Click on the information to close the tooltip.
